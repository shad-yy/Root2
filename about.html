About SMART Live TV

Your ultimate destination for live sports streaming, real-time scores, and in-depth sports analysis.

Our Story

SMART Live TV was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate sports enthusiasts who were tired of missing their favorite sporting events due to regional restrictions and complicated subscription services.

What started as a small project quickly grew into a comprehensive sports streaming platform, dedicated to providing fans around the world with high-quality, reliable access to live sports events.

Today, SMART Live TV serves millions of users globally, offering seamless streaming of football, UFC/MMA, Formula 1, boxing, and more, all in one place.

Our Mission & Values

Global Access

We believe sports should be accessible to fans everywhere, regardless of geographic location.

Real-Time Experience

We're committed to providing lightning-fast streams and up-to-the-second scores and statistics.

Community First

We prioritize building a community of passionate sports fans who can connect through their shared interests.

Continuous Innovation

We constantly improve our platform with new features, better performance, and expanded coverage.
