Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for SMART Live TV?

To sign up for SMART Live TV, click the "Sign Up" button at the top of our homepage. You can choose from several subscription options, enter your details, and start streaming immediately after payment.

What devices can I watch SMART Live TV on?

SMART Live TV is available on a wide range of devices including web browsers, iOS and Android mobile devices, smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and gaming consoles (PlayStation, Xbox).

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by logging into your account, going to "Account Settings," and selecting "Manage Subscription." Follow the cancellation instructions. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Is SMART Live TV available worldwide?

Yes, SMART Live TV is available in most countries worldwide. However, some content may be region-restricted due to broadcasting rights. Check our coverage page for specific details about content availability in your region.

How can I improve my streaming quality?

For the best streaming experience, we recommend an internet connection of at least 10 Mbps for HD and 25 Mbps for 4K content. Using a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi can also improve stability. Make sure to close other applications that might be using bandwidth while streaming.
